Introduction

Welcome to Salesforce OmniStudio Helper, a Chrome extension designed to enhance your Salesforce OmniStudio experience. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to keep it secure.

Data Collection

Salesforce OmniStudio Helper collects and stores the following types of data:

  • Favorite Records: Information about the records you mark as favorites within Salesforce.
  • Current Org Namespace: The namespace of the current Salesforce organization you are working in.
  • Extension Settings: Preferences and settings related to the functionality of the extension.

All data is stored locally on your browser using Chrome's local storage.

Data Usage

The data collected by Salesforce OmniStudio Helper is used solely for the purpose of providing and enhancing the functionality of the extension. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Provide quick access to your favorite records.
  • Maintain the correct namespace context for your Salesforce organization.
  • Remember your extension settings and preferences to personalize your experience.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties. All data remains on your local machine and is not transmitted to any external servers or services.

Data Security

We implement various security measures to ensure that your data is protected. These measures include:

  • Using Chrome's local storage to keep your data within your control.
  • Following best practices for secure coding to prevent unauthorized access or data breaches.

User Control

You have full control over the data stored by Salesforce OmniStudio Helper. You can view, edit, or delete your data at any time through the extension's settings. If you choose to uninstall the extension, all data stored locally will be deleted automatically.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes through the extension interface or via email, if you have provided one.
